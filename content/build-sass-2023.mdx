Sometimes, it can be challenging to determine which tools are suitable for building a SaaS or MVP. Here is a list of tools that you can use or try out in 2023. These tools are highly beneficial and widely appreciated by many developers.

- **Web Framework**: [Next.js](https://nextjs.org/)
- **UI & Component Library:** [Shadcn/ui](https://ui.shadcn.com/) and [radix.ui](https://www.radix-ui.com/)
- **Styling**: [TailwindCSS](https://tailwindcss.com/)
- **Charts:** [Tinybird](https://www.tinybird.co/) and [Tremor](https://www.tremor.so/)
- **Database:** [PlanetScale](https://planetscale.com/) or [Supabase](https://supabase.com/)
- **Emails**: [Resend](https://resend.com/)
- **Payments**: [Stripe](https://stripe.com/)
- **Auth**: [NextAuth.js](https://next-auth.js.org/)
- **Deployment**: [Vercel](https://vercel.com/)
- **Blogging**: [Hashnode](https://hashnode.com/)
- **E2E:** [Replay.io](https://www.replay.io/)
